Our Mission

At PatchThis, we believe that software security shouldn't be a barrier to innovation. We're building the world's most intelligent patching platform to help organizations stay secure without compromising productivity.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PatchThis emerged from the frustration of dealing with complex, error-prone patching processes in enterprise environments. Our founders, seasoned cybersecurity professionals, recognized that traditional patching methods were too slow, too risky, and too resource-intensive.

Today, PatchThis serves thousands of organizations worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them maintain security compliance while focusing on their core business objectives.
